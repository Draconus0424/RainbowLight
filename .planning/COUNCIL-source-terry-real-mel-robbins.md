## Core Principles

### 1. Wise Adult vs Adaptive Child

"Which part of you am I speaking to? Prefrontal cortex -- wise adult. When you get trauma triggered, flooded -- adaptive child takes over. Fight, flight, or fawn (fix)."

"Other therapies teach you skills. What I've created deals with the part of you that won't use them."

### 2. One-Up / One-Down

"One-up: grandiose, attacking, righteous, boundary-less. One-down: fixing, pleasing, accommodating, resentful. Both are adaptive child. Both make a mess."

"If you're small, get big. If you're big, get small."

### 3. We All Marry Our Unfinished Business

"Your partner is exquisitely designed to stick the burning spear right into your eyeball. That's not a bad relationship. That's a real relationship."

### 4. Harmony, Disharmony, Repair

"All relationships are an endless dance of harmony, disharmony, and repair. Most couples are in harmony and disharmony. But no repair. Because that's where the skills come in."

### 5. No Harshness

"There is no redeeming value whatsoever in harshness. Harshness does nothing that loving firmness doesn't do better."

### 6. Three Steps to Getting What You Want

1. Dare to rock the boat -- tell the truth
2. Teach your partner what you want
3. Reward them when they do it (even half-assed)

### 7. Relational Mindfulness

"When you're flooded, take a break. Walk around the block. Take 10 breaths. You have about 10 seconds of tolerance for the wound before you move into your automatic habitual response."

### 8. The Healing Comes When They DON'T Give It to You

"The healing doesn't come when you get them to give it to you. The healing comes when they don't and you deal with it not the way you learned as a kid, but in a new way."

### 9. Relational vs Individual Empowerment

"I was weak, now I'm strong. Go screw yourself -- that's individual empowerment. I was weak, now I'm strong. Let's work together -- that's relational empowerment."

### 10. Miserable/Comfortable vs Happy/Uncomfortable

"Your adaptive child is safe and comfortable. You could do it in your sleep. Wise adult is new, courageous, scary."
